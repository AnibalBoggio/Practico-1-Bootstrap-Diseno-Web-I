NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 4090

The flagship of Ada Lovelace RTX 4000 series . Powered by a staggering 16,384 CUDA cores, with a maximum clock speed of 2.53 GHz, 128 3rd Generation RT Cores and 512 4th Generation Tensor Cores. It features a massive 24GB GDDR6X of VRAM with a 384-bit bus, offering a staggering 1008GB/s bandwidth. Prepare for maximum productivity and gaming excellence. Experience cutting-edge DLSS 3 Frame Generation technology and unlimited Ray Tracing performance.

AMD Radeon™ RX 7900 XTX

RX 7900 XTX stands as the pinnacle of the AMD RDNA 3 RX 7000 series. Driven by 6144 Stream Processors, with a maximum clock speed of 2.5 GHz and 96 Ray Acelerators. It offers 24GB GDDR6 of VRAM with a 384-bit bus, offering a upto 960GB/s. Enjoy the AMD Infinity Cache Technology and AMD Smart Access Memory further elevate gaming excellence, all at an attractive price point.

Intel® Arc™ A770

The currently crown jewel of Intel, from the Alchemist architecture series. It is impulsed by 32 Xe Cores at a maximum clock speed of 2.1 GHz, 8 Render Segment, 32 Ray Tracing Units and 512 XMX Engines. It cointains the unenviable amount of 16GB GDDR6 of VRAM with a 256-bit bus, offering a memory bandwidth of 560GB/s. Experience Intel XeSS rescaling technology, Xe Media Engine and Xe Display Engine. It's not a top-tier GPU but its performance per dollar is one of the best.

AI Upscaling Technologies

XeSS (Xe Super Sampling)

XeSS stands out for its association with Intel and their integrated graphics solutions. It aims to provide efficient upscaling utilizing Intel's hardware, enhancing performance and visual quality, especially for users with Intel graphics processors.

DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling)

DLSS is notable for its use of deep learning neural networks developed by NVIDIA. Its strength lies in its ability to dynamically adapt and enhance graphics quality, offering significant performance gains while maintaining impressive visual fidelity in supported games.

FSR (FidelityFX Super Resolution)

FSR distinguishes itself with its vendor-agnostic approach by AMD. Unlike XeSS and DLSS tied to specific manufacturers, FSR is compatible with a wide range of GPUs, making it a versatile solution for improving gaming performance and image quality across different hardware setups.
